About

What is Heroes Uprising?

Heroes Uprising is a charming virtual realm brimming with adorable yet powerful beings known as Heroes, who come equipped with their own Swords. These Heroes, along with their Swords, can be engaged in battles, forged, and collected, all while earning valuable resources and collectibles that can be traded on an open marketplace.

This immersive interoperable game was created not only for entertainment, but also to showcase the innovative potential of Blockchain & AI Technology in a fun and nostalgic way.








Heroes

What is a Hero?

Heroes are summoned by players to assist them in their journey through the land of Mydoslyne. These summoned heroes will aid you in your quest, but each element has its own set of strengths and weaknesses, so choose your hero wisely and build your team to your liking.

Heroes will be the one who wields the swords. Without Heroes, swords will be useless in the game.






Swords

What is a Sword?

The Heroes wields swords in order to combat foes and increase their chances of success, with the effectiveness of the weapon being determined by the Heroes' possession of the corresponding attribute. Given that all parts are integral components of the sword, a Hero alone cannot suffice for gameplay.






Sprites

What is a Sprite?

In Anglevin, a distinct realm, Sprites exist and have been summoned to assist heroes in their battles, drawing on their inherent magical capabilities to bolster their bonded individuals. The power of each Sprite is linked to the quality of its magic and its specific elemental affinity.
